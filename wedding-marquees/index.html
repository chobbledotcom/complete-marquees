Wedding Marquee Hire

At Complete Marquees, we specialise in wedding marquee hire and would love to help make your day unforgettable. Whether you are planning a wedding, birthday party, christening, garden party, or a small family gathering, we have a marquee to suit your occasion.

Perfect For:

  • Wedding Marquee Hire
  • Party Marquee Hire
  • Corporate Event Marquee Hire
  • Fun Day Event Marquee Hire
  • Garden Parties of any size

Since 2002, our family-run business has delivered hundreds of events across Hampshire, Surrey, and West Sussex. We provide stylish modern outdoor shelters, complete with lighting and furnishings to enhance your event.

Our versatile Capri marquees can be linked together, allowing you to cover large or irregular outdoor areas. This flexibility makes them perfect for weddings and other celebrations. You can customise your marquee with lighting, décor, and seating arrangements to match your theme.

At Complete Marquees, we pride ourselves on service, reliability, and punctuality. Our experienced team ensures your marquee arrives on time, is professionally installed, and ready well before your event begins.

To see examples of our work, view our marquee gallery. You can also check our price and package lists to find a solution that fits your event and budget.
