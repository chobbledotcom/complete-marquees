Wedding & Event Marquee Hire

At Complete Marquees, we specialise in stylish marquee hire for weddings, parties, corporate events, garden celebrations, and more. Serving Hampshire, Surrey, and West Sussex, our family-run business has delivered hundreds of successful events since 2002.

Capri & Pagoda Marquees

Our modern Capri marquees offer a fresh alternative to traditional styles. They come in multiple sizes and can be linked together to cover large or irregular outdoor areas. This flexibility makes them perfect for weddings, corporate functions, or fun days. The Pagoda marquees feature a striking pitched roof and can be used alone or as an entrance to a larger marquee. Both options provide elegant, functional spaces for any occasion.

Equipment hire & Packages

We offer fully customisable packages. You can add or remove items to suit your event. Choose from seating arrangements, plain marquees with dance floors, or fully furnished setups. Enhance your marquee with lighting, flooring, and furniture to create a stylish, professional environment. You can even hire a stage for live bands or entertainment.

Additional Entertainment

Looking for extra fun? Our sister site, Monster Event Hire, offers hundreds of activities, from inflatables to photo booths, making your event even more memorable.

Why Choose Complete Marquees?

We pride ourselves on reliability, punctuality, and professional service. Our experienced team handles delivery, installation, and collection, ensuring your event runs smoothly. Whether you are planning a small garden party or a large wedding, we provide everything you need to make it unforgettable.
