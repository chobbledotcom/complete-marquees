## Why This Matters

Marquee hire is a high-trust purchase. Customers are handing over deposits months in advance for a structure they cannot inspect, that has to arrive on a fixed day, withstand weather, and be safe around their guests. The market is full of one-van operators who fold between seasons — losing deposits, missing dates, or putting up unsafe structures. Every credential below answers the unspoken question: *"How do I know you'll actually turn up, do it properly, and still be here next year?"*
