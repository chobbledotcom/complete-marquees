Please see below for answers to many frequently asked questions on our FAQ’s-complete-marquees page. If you can’t find the answer you’re looking for, please be sure to contact our team who are happy to help on 01428751745.

Do the marquees come with side walls?

A- Yes all marquee’s come with side walls as standard, half clear, half solid walls. On the day of set up, you can choose if you would like to have them on or off, or could even have half on half on.  

Can I decorate the marquee myself?

A- You are more than welcome to decorate the marquees.  you would need to use string no sticky tape and staples when putting up your decorations, also no crepe paper as this does stain the marquee.

Are Capri marquees weatherproof?

A- Yes they are waterproof, please note not in stormy weather.

Can you connect a Capri marquee to a building?

A-  I’m afraid we cannot connect these types of marquees to buildings due to there shape.

How high are the Capri marquee arches?

A: They are approximately 7ft to 8ft high you would have plenty of head space, the arches creating a more open feel in the marquee. They are approx. 16ft high at the centre pole.

How much space do we require?

A- We have four sizes available, however larger sizes can be made by joining two or more marquees together. Capri marquees can be linked together to form many interesting shapes and sizes. Please contact us for further details to help with this.

Do Capri marquees have to go onto a grassed area?

A- All sizes booked must be able to fit on a flat grassed area, with NO TREES, BUSHES OR HEDGES running through the marquee site, the client MUST check any space for the size ordered.

Do we need to have flooring?

A: No during the summer depending on the grass condition, no flooring is needed, but this does also .  If you would like a Dance floor in the marquee, we do then have to put flooring down to protect the wooden dance floor.

What Is A Capri Marquee? Capri marquee is simply a different style of a marquee. A far cry from the traditional rectangles and squares with 4 walls and an apex ceiling, a Capri marquee is what you need when you want to make a real statement. They are wonderfully unusual, extremely eye-catching and very versatile. Most easily recognised by their distinctive multi-peaked ceiling, equipped with matting and archway lighting and work perfectly as an open space or enclosed by PVC sidings. They can come in a range of sizes too.
