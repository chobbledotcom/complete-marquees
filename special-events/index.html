Marquee Hire for Special Events

Perfect For:

  • Weddings

Complete Marquees is a modern, family-run marquee hire company serving Surrey and surrounding areas. We specialise in providing high-quality marquees for a wide range of events, from intimate garden parties to large corporate gatherings.

If you are looking to hire a marquee for your special event, you’ve come to the right place. Our contemporary Capri marquees offer a fresh and modern alternative to traditional styles. You can link multiple Capri marquees together, giving you flexibility to create spaces of any size or layout.

We offer a wide selection of packages to suit your needs and budget. Whether you need a small setup for a private party or a large marquee for a corporate event, we can provide a solution tailored to your requirements. Our team will help with delivery, setup, and collection, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience.

Capri marquees also lend themselves beautifully to décor and lighting, allowing you to customise your event space to match your theme. Guests will enjoy a stylish, professional, and inviting atmosphere, whether it’s a wedding, fun day, or corporate function.

Take a look at our marquees in action and explore our gallery for inspiration. We also cover a wide range of locations throughout Surrey and Hampshire, making it easy to find a marquee hire solution near you.
