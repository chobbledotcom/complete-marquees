Marquee Hire in Hampshire

Looking for marquee hire in Hampshire? Complete Marquees has been providing stylish, reliable, and fully customisable marquees since 2002. We cater to weddings, corporate events, fun days, and garden parties of all sizes. As a family-run business, we pride ourselves on creating memorable outdoor events with professional service.

Our Capri marquees are flexible and can be linked together to cover large outdoor areas. Whether you’re planning a small garden party or a large corporate function, our marquees provide elegant shelter, professional lighting, and stylish furnishings. Every event looks polished and runs smoothly with our support.

We offer a range of marquee packages to suit different occasions. You can browse our marquee range to see images of past events or check our price and package list to find the right setup. All packages can be customised with flooring, lighting, furniture, and décor to match your style and event needs.

To avoid disappointment, we recommend checking date availability early. Our marquees are popular, especially during weddings and summer months. Booking in advance ensures you get the right size and package for your event.

At Complete Marquees, we handle everything—from delivery and professional setup to safe breakdown. This allows you to relax and enjoy your event while we take care of the logistics.
